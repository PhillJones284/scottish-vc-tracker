Finding the Crunchbase authcookie in DevTools:

1. Go to the Crunchbase page and open DevTools (Cmd+Option+I)
2. Network tab → refresh the page → type v4/data in the filter box
3. Click any request in the list → click the Cookies tab in the right panel
4. Find authcookie — copy its value (the long eyJ... JWT string, not the authcookie= prefix)
